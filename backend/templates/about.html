What is Online Government Document System?

Online Government Document System is an online portal set up in order to provide services to residents of Ahmedabad. This portal provides two types of certificates Income Certificate and Non Creamy Layer Certificate All the services will be covered in a section later. Here,we are going to tell you everything you need to know about Digital Gujarat and how it can benefit you.

What are the Benefits of Using Government Document Portal?

  • Availing of services.
  • All the services come under a single portal.
  • Easy and quick user registration.
  • Everything is done online and the delivery of services is really fast.

    • What are the Requirements to Register in Digital Gujarat Portal?

    Any person who is a citizen of Ahmedabad can easily register on the Government Documents portal and benefit from its services. However, if you are not a citizen of Ahmedabad, you will not be able to take advantage of these services because these services are available for the people of Ahmedabad only. If you are a citizen of Ahmedabad, then you just need to register on the portal and you can use any service you wish to.


    When you register with the portal then you need to provide your personal information such as name,contact number,e-mail id and any information that may be required by us for allowing you to access for availing the services.
